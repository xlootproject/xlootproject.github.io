What is xLoot?

What is xLoot?

Extension Loot - known as xLoot - is a collection of 7,777 unique bags of adventurer gear that extends the original idea of Loot. xLoot was released by Colin Platt (@colingplatt). xLoot is an extension of the idea started with Loot - an open and free to mint NFT bag of gear for adventurers. Each xLoot bag contains 8 items: a piece for an adventurer's chest, foot, hand, head, neck, ring, waist, and weapon. xLoot is more than just an NFT project, its a community. It requires the community to build onto it in an bottom-up fashion, creating a decentralized metaverse all tied together through NFT ownership.

Info / FAQ

Is xLoot a part of Loot or developed by the same team?

No - both Loot and xLoot are fully decentralized, community owned and driven projects. There is no official team for Loot or xLoot. The original developers do not have any obligation to add-on or grow the project in any way. Loot was designed to be an open-source NFT toolkit or blueprint, to be iterated on and implemented in projects by the community, not by a core team. xLoot's author, Colin, loved the original Loot concept but wanted to make the project accessible to more people so he created xLoot. xLoot respects the original Loot thesis and also the original Token ID's of Loot, so it was not just a simple clone. The xLoot community today is a thriving hub of talent and innovation. Our community is bringing together, artists, writers, developers and projects managers to create amazing products or projects that are based on the xLoot NFT's - driving more value to those who hold xLoot bags.

What makes xLoot valuable?

xLoot is designed as a foundation for a community governed, bottom-up, decentralized metaverse. Projects that implement xLoot NFTs and the community around these projects is what ultimately drives its value.

What can I use xLoot for?

Anything you want! Just like Loot, xLoot was launched with no defined rarity, stats, visual assets or additional functionality. Feel free to use xLoot in any way that you want. Check out what our community is building under Resources.

How rare is my xLoot bag?

Items do have varying levels of how often they occur in the series of xLoot bags, but true rarity is determined by the community and the way xLoot is implemented in other projects. An example that carried over from the Loot community was that Divine Robes and Katanas were more highly valued than other items, but that can change at any point depending on the community's sentiment. You can learn more about occurences and raritie calculations in our Resources.
